About Levite Family

Levite Family is a gospel ensemble formed to lead worship and uplift communities through heartfelt songs and ministry. We combine traditional hymns with contemporary arrangements to create an atmosphere of praise suitable for churches, events and festivals.

Our Mission

To worship God through music, disciple listeners, and use songs to bring hope and restoration.

Our Vision

To be a household name in gospel music that ministers to generations.

Core Values

Integrity • Worship • Community • Excellence
